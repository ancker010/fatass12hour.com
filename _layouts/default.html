COVID-19 UPDATE

Until last week, I was planning to cancel Big Jay's Ultra Cycling Challenge for 2020. Coronavirus, a lack or Rotary volunteers, travel restrictions in Illinois and businesses being forced to close their doors were all factors working against the race. With the absence of any races or even group rides, my training had dwindled to a point where I was considering myself a recreational cyclist and I'm certain I wasn't the only one who had this issue. However, a friend of mine (who is instrumental in the bike race), contacted me last week and asked what was going to happen to the race. I told him I was planning to cancel, but felt terrible about it. He told me that moving the race back to the original mid-July date would help everybody. Not only would it give the ultra-cycling community a badly needed reason to train, but it would also help the good people of Nepal. Hearing this, I reconsidered everything. As of April 30, Big Jay's Ultra Cycling Challenge will take place on July 18. The start location may change, but will stay in Mahomet with the same format as the previous 11 years. However, if restrictions/group meetings are still in place one month prior to the race (June 18), it will be cancelled for 2020. So, check out the website for the latest info. Please contact me with comments or questions.

Ride! Ride! Ride! Big Jay Yost

Frequently Asked Questions

Q -

I have to XYZ, can I start earlier/later than the posted start times?

A -

No. It's impossible to track anything other than everyone starting and finishing at the posted start/finish times.

Q -

Will the race start on time?

A -

Yes, and please attend the pre-race briefing at 5:50 and 11:50.

Q -

Can I start early? No. These are timed events.

A -

Anyone starting before 12:00 will be racing the 12 Hour race, while anyone starting after 12:00 will be racing the 6 Hour event.

Q -

Is this race really free?

A -

Yes. But the volunteers are from the local Rotary Club, so please make a tax-deductible donation. We encourage $25.

Q -

Is there a rain date?

A -

No.

Q -

What if I have a problem on the route?

A -

Sit tight. We have a SAG Wagon that will get to you.

Q -

Is there a pre-registration?

A -

Not really. Please let us know if you plan on racing, but registration is so quick and easy, there is no need for pre-registration.

Q -

Do you offer chip timing?

A -

No. You are completely 'on your honor' for this event.

Q -

If I am too tired to continue, can I ride backwards/short-cut the course?

A -

No. The only time you will be authorized to ride backwards on the route is in the last hour/closing minutes.
